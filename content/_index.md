I live by the motto "Let's live beauty!" and believe:

- Justice is inevitable.
- Curiosity is essential.
- Collaboration is vital.
- The act of understanding can make the world more excellent.
- Science and art are complementary means of understanding experience through reason and intuition.
- For the enterprise of science to succeed, the methods and means to reproduce and test claims is required.
- For the enterprise of art to succeed, expression must be rooted in tradition and grow with new technologies.
- Coaxing art from utility reveals hidden beauty.
- Doing is a good way to learn; doing together is better.
- Code, communities, and cultures are living systems that learn.
- Good tools serve people, so try to make good tools.

Now go forth and be excellent!
